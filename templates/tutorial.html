How to Use This Website

Follow these simple steps to organize your documents efficiently.

Step 1: Upload Your Document

Click on the upload box and select a file or drag and drop your document (images, DOCX files, etc.).

Step 2: Scanning the Text

Our system will scan the uploaded file to extract text and identify its content.

Step 3: Categorizing the Document

The extracted text is analyzed and assigned to a relevant category based on its content.

Step 4: Organizing into Folders

Once categorized, your document is saved into a folder corresponding to its category.

Step 5: Access Your Organized Documents

You can access your documents anytime by navigating through the categorized folders.
